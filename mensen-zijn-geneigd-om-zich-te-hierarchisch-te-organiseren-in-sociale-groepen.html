Mensen zijn geneigd om zich te hiërarchisch te organiseren in sociale groepen

OdinAangemaakt op:4th May 2021 at 2:09pm - Laatst geëdit op:4th May 2021 at 2:13pm
Zettel

Regardless of a society’s form of government, the contents of its fundamental belief system, or the complexity of its social and economic arrangements, human societies tend to organise as group-based social hierarchies in which at least one group enjoys greater social status and power than other groups

(Pratto, Felicia, et al. 2006)
Pratto, Felicia and Sidanius, Jim and Levin, Shana. Social dominance theory and the dynamics of intergroup relations: Taking stock and looking forward. (2006)
